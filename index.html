Project 01

Melanin HR Analysis & Dashboard

This project explored workforce data through an HR lens, with a focus on representation and equity. Working entirely in Microsoft Excel, I walked through the full analytics process, from importing and cleaning raw HR records to structuring the data for meaningful analysis.

I used Excel formulas, pivot tables, and conditional logic to surface patterns across key metrics including headcount, department distribution, and demographic breakdowns. The analysis was designed to answer a simple but important question: what does the data actually say about who's in the room?

The findings were brought to life through an interactive Excel dashboard, featuring charts and summary visuals that make the story easy to follow at a glance. This project sharpened my ability to work within tool constraints while still delivering clean, actionable insights and reminded me that powerful analysis doesn't always require powerful software.
